Delicious Veggie Pizza

This is a delicious and easy vegitable pizza recipe. You will love it.

Ingredients

  • Mushroom
  • Onion
  • Peppers
  • Olives
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Crust
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Set out the crust
  3. Spread the pizza sauce on your crust
  4. Spread the Mozzerella Cheese on top of the sauce
  5. Spread the Mushroom on top of the cheese
  6. Spread the Onion on top of the cheese
  7. Spread the Peppers on top of the cheese
  8. Spread the Olives on top of the cheese
  9. Sprinkle Parmesan Cheese on top of the entire pizza
  10. Put the pizza directly on the center rack
  11. Bake for 20 minutes.

Enjoy

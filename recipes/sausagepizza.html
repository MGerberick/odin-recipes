Delicious Sausage Pizza

This is a delicious and easy Sausage pizza recipe. You will love it.

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Crust
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Set out the crust
  3. Spread the pizza sauce on your crust
  4. Spread the Mozzerella Cheese on top of the sauce
  5. Spread the Sausage on top of the cheese
  6. Sprinkle Parmesan Cheese on top of the entire pizza
  7. Put the pizza directly on the center rack
  8. Bake for 20 minutes.

Enjoy

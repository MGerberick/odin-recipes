Delicious Cheese Pizza

This is a delicious and easy Cheese pizza recipe. You will love it.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Sauce
  • Crust
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Pre-heat oven to 425 degrees
  2. Set out the crust
  3. Spread the pizza sauce on your crust
  4. Spread the Mozzerella Cheese on top of the sauce
  5. Sprinkle Parmesan Cheese on top of the entire pizza
  6. Put the pizza directly on the center rack
  7. Bake for 20 minutes.

Enjoy
